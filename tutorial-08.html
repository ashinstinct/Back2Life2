What You'll Create

A single unbroken 15-second desk-cam shot — the camera never moves. A tiny doll-like figure named Lisa stands frozen on a blank Photoshop canvas on an ASUS monitor. She suddenly comes to life, smashes through the screen's liquid-like surface, and lands on the keyboard below, breaking the keycaps as she delivers a direct-to-camera sales pitch. A giant robotic fist then punches through the screen, grabs her, and drags her back through the portal — the screen settles back to calm, frozen stillness, like nothing happened.
