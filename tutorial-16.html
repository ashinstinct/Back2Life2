Escape
The Temple

A 15-shot, 15-second Indiana Jones-style temple raid — golden idol swap, collapsing corridors, a giant boulder chase, and a rain-soaked victory finale — built shot-by-shot with Seedance 2 Mini.

Tool: Seedance 2 Mini
Quality: 720p · 16:9
Difficulty: Advanced
Time: ~25 mins
Follow Instagram TikTok YouTube

What You'll Create

A full Raiders of the Lost Ark-style temple raid told as 15 distinct shots, one per second. Your explorer enters a hidden Peruvian jungle temple at sunrise, swaps a golden idol for a sandbag, triggers the entire temple's trap system, and sprints through poison darts, a collapsing bridge, a swinging blade, and a giant boulder — before diving out into the rain as the temple collapses behind him. Each shot is labeled and built independently, then stitched into one continuous adventure sequence.

▸Character sheet locks identical face, stubble, and outfit across all 15 shots
▸Anamorphic 16:9 widescreen, Hollywood adventure cinematography
▸Torch lighting, drifting dust, and humid jungle atmosphere throughout
▸Golden idol swap and trap-trigger sequence
▸Boulder chase, collapsing bridge, and swinging blade action beats
▸Rain-soaked victory finale with the temple collapsing behind him

Prepare Your Reference Photo

A clear photo is the foundation this whole generation is built on

1

Choose a clear, well-lit photo of yourself (or whoever's appearing in the clip). Front-facing works best — avoid sunglasses or heavy shadows.

2

Turn it into a multi-angle reference sheet using GPT Image 2 — four angles plus a face close-up on a plain background, exactly like the one below. This is the same method used to build the reference sheet for this generation.

3

Open Dreamina AI and start a new Seedance 2 Mini image-to-video generation.

4

Upload your reference sheet as the reference / character image.
