The Yellow Superhero
Awakening

Four reference images, one continuous shot: a woman in a yellow hoodie sleeps in a golden-hour park, a monster rampages in the distance, she stomps her foot — golden armor assembles from the ground up, shoulder cannons deploy, and the park returns to calm like nothing happened.

Tool: Seedance 2.0
References: 4 images
Difficulty: Advanced
Time: ~25 mins
Follow Instagram TikTok YouTube

What You'll Create

This prompt orchestrates four reference images in one continuous shot — @image_1 locks the woman, @image_2 locks the park location, @image_3 locks the golden robot design, and @image_4 locks the monster. That's the ceiling of what Seedance reference mentions can do, and the writing shows how to keep it coherent: the camera is the narrator, whip-panning between the sleeping woman and the distant monster so both exist in the same world before the transformation connects them. The ending is the masterstroke — armor dissolves, she checks her phone, the park goes calm. The anticlimax is the hook.

▸A four-reference continuous shot: character, location, robot form, and monster
▸A ground-up golden armor transformation triggered by a foot stomp
▸Dual shoulder-cannon barrage with tracers, shell casings and chained explosions
▸A calm anticlimax ending that makes the clip endlessly rewatchable
▸Both the full prompt and a short version for stricter character limits

Prepare Your Four Reference Images

Each @ mention needs a matching upload

1

@image_1 — the character: a full-body photo of the woman (or your Lisa character) in a yellow hoodie and yellow trousers on a clean background. Yellow wardrobe matters — the payoff of the reveal is her returning to the same outfit.

2

@image_2 — the location: a photo of a park bench among trees. A real photo works; so does a generated golden-hour park scene.

3

@image_3 — the robot form: a golden armored robot design with dual shoulder-mounted cannons and a glowing visor. Generate it to echo the character's yellow palette so the transformation reads as the same person.

4

@image_4 — the monster: a hulking creature design on a plain background. Keep it visually distinct from the robot (organic vs mechanical) so the model never blends them.

5

Upload all four in order so the mentions map correctly on platforms that number references automatically.

💡

Pro tip: Colour-link the character and the robot (yellow hoodie → gold armor) but contrast the monster. Reference sets that share a palette across ALL images cause identity bleed between the robot and the creature.

📋 Full Prompt
Single continuous cinematic shot in a quiet urban park @image_2 during golden hour, warm sunlight filtering through tall trees, soft wind rustling leaves, a wooden bench in the foreground where a young woman in a yellow hoodie @image_1 sleeps peacefully, her chest rising gently, hair slightly moving in the breeze, calm and serene atmosphere.

a deep rumble builds, the ground subtly vibrates, leaves tremble and fall, birds suddenly scatter into the sky reacting to the disturbance, the camera smoothly whip-pans forward away from the woman, shifting focus into the ahead of her, a monstrous creature @image_4 is seen rampaging people running, partially obscured by heat haze and dust, stomping heavily, cracking pavement, sending debris and dirt into the air, its glowing eyes and aggressive movements clearly visible despite the distance.

the camera holds briefly on the monster as it roars and thrashes, then quickly but smoothly pans back toward the woman on the bench, restoring her as the focal point — she awakens and sit on the bench, while she stand up lowering slightly into a closeup low-angle — she forcefully stomps her foot onto the ground, sending a visible ripple through dirt and scattered leaves

from the impact point, transformation begins — metallic structures rapidly form over her foot, spreading upward in a seamless morph, golden armor assembling with mechanical precision, sparks and energy pulses flickering — the close-up camera cranes upward following the transformation as it envelops her legs, torso, arms, and finally her head, locking into a sleek robotic helmet with a glowing visor now fully transformed into a golden armored robot @image_3 with dual shoulder-mounted cannons, the close-up camera arcs around her into a dynamic three-quarter angle, wind intensifies, dust and leaves swirl dramatically, sunlight glinting off polished metal

she locks onto the distant monster — her shoulders rotate as the cannons deploy with mechanical precision — the camera pushes slightly forward as both cannons unleash continuous rapid-fire machine gun bursts, relentless streams of bullets firing in high-speed succession, intense strobing muzzle flashes flicker rhythmically, ejecting shell casings scattering through the air, recoil vibrations subtly shaking her upper body, shockwaves rippling outward with each burst

tracer rounds streak across the park in dense volleys, tearing through the air toward the monster — impacts land in rapid succession, violently ripping into the creature as explosions chain together across its body, dust, debris, and fragments erupting continuously — the monster attempts to charge but is overwhelmed by the sustained barrage the barrage intensifies into a final overwhelming stream — culminating in a massive explosive detonation that engulfs the monster completely, a fireball expanding outward, sending debris flying and briefly overexposing the frame

as the smoke clears, the camera eases back to toward the robot upper body, stabilizing — she then relaxes — the armor retracts in reverse, dissolving into glowing particles, revealing the woman once again in her yellow outfit she then sit back to the bench near her, sits down and pick her phone from pocket and start using the phone as if nothing happened — the park returns to calm, leaves gently falling, sunlight warm and peaceful — the camera keep focus on upper body.
