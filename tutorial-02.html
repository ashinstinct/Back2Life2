Create Your Character Sheet

Use Nano Banana Pro in Higgsfield to generate a multi-angle reference sheet from a selfie

1

Go to Higgsfield.ai and tap Create Image. Select the Nano Banana Pro model.

2

Upload a clear, well-lit selfie as your reference image. Front-facing works best — avoid sunglasses or heavy shadows.

3

Use the prompt below to generate a full four-angle character sheet with face closeup. This is your identity reference for the video generation.

📋 Prompt — Step 1: Character Sheet
Character reference sheet, white background, four views of the same man: front facing, three-quarter left, back view, three-quarter right — plus one large face closeup portrait on the right side. Battle-worn middle-aged man, silver cropped hair, grey stubble beard, olive green casual shirt, dark jeans, white sneakers. Clean product photography lighting. Turnaround sheet style.
💡

Pro tip: Enable Unlimited mode and 2K quality in Nano Banana Pro for the sharpest character sheet. The more detail in this reference, the better Seedance 2.0 will reproduce your face in the video.

Example Character Sheet — Generated with Nano Banana Pro
Four-angle turnaround plus face closeup. This exact image was used as the character reference in Step 3.
4

Download the character sheet once you're happy with the result. You'll upload this in Step 3 as your Seedance reference image.

Prepare Your Animal Reference

Optional but recommended — a photo of the animal you want to transform into a battle creature

1

Find or take a clear photo of your chosen animal. In this example we used a fluffy grey-and-white Persian/Exotic Shorthair kitten with a flat face.

2

The animal's colouring and markings will be preserved in the battle-creature transformation — so a distinctive-looking animal makes for a more striking result.

3

You can also skip the photo and describe the animal purely in the prompt — Seedance will generate it from your description.

Set Up Seedance 2.0

Upload your character sheet reference and configure the generation settings

1

In Higgsfield.ai, tap Create Video and select Seedance 2.0 Fast.

2

Tap the image upload / reference button and upload your character sheet from Step 1. Set the role to Reference Image or Omni.

3

Configure your generation settings using the table below before pasting the prompt.

Important: This is a single continuous shot prompt — no cuts or edits. Do not trim or edit the output. The prompt is engineered to tell Seedance 2.0 exactly how the camera moves, so what you get is the final video.

4

Paste the Prompt & Generate

The full cinematic prompt — copy exactly as written for best results

1

Copy the full prompt below and paste it into the Seedance 2.0 prompt field. Do not shorten or paraphrase it — the length and specificity is intentional.

2

Replace "the fluffy grey-and-white flat-faced Persian kitten" with your own animal description if you're using a different creature.

3

Tap Generate. At 480p Fast the generation typically takes 2–4 minutes.

📋 Full Seedance 2.0 Prompt — Paste Exactly As Written
ONE single continuous unbroken camera shot — zero cuts, zero edits, zero angle changes. The camera floats, orbits, pushes, and pulls dynamically but never cuts.

The camera opens high in the sky looking down as the man falls backwards through the air screaming, legs kicking wildly, clutching the fluffy grey-and-white flat-faced Persian kitten tightly to his chest — casual olive green shirt and dark jeans. The ruined white marble temple rushes up far below. The camera falls with him tracking the descent until he slams into the temple floor with a thunderous shockwave explosion of stone dust and debris, rolling hard across the ground protecting the cat. The camera floats down and around him as he rises into a half-kneeling position — one knee on the ground, one foot flat — cat tucked under his left arm, right hand raising a glowing golden crystal shard to eye level. The camera slowly pushes in toward his face keeping the cat fully visible in frame beside him as he examines the crystal. Both the man and the cat slowly turn their heads toward each other. He smirks and says: Ok, are you ready? The camera holds this two-shot — both faces visible — as he turns back and grips the crystal with his fist. The camera pushes closer as his eyes ignite with blazing solid gold light, crystal light blazing between his clenched fingers. A sonic boom shockwave of energy detonates outward in a ring. The camera pulls back wide in a smooth continuous arc keeping both man and cat in frame simultaneously as white-and-gold angel plate armor snaps onto the man piece by piece with crackling energy — chest plate slamming on, shoulders locking in, gauntlets clicking — while at the exact same time beside him the fluffy grey-and-white kitten on the ground begins to pulse with golden light and rapidly grow and morph, fur expanding, body stretching, flat face widening into a massive mean powerful battle-lion with the same grey tabby and white coloring — both transformations happening together in the same continuous frame. The camera orbits around them both as the man screams, massive white feathered wings ripping from his back unfurling wide, and he draws two glowing golden swords. The battle-lion beside him throws back its huge grey-and-white head and roars, shaking the broken columns. The camera sweeps low and accelerates forward as both man and lion charge directly at the lens together, a blinding explosion of golden energy and white light obliterating the frame.

Setting: Colossal ruined white marble temple, cracked columns, ancient stone carvings, golden god-rays, warm amber light throughout.

Audio: Wind rush and screaming on the fall, thunderous impact, stone dust settling, soft tense silence as he examines the crystal, deep bass pulse as the crystal activates, crackling energy building, sonic boom detonation, metallic armor-snap impacts one by one, lion transformation roar building underneath, full orchestral swell, wings ripping sound, two sword draws, lion full roar shaking the air, choir hit, epic percussion as they charge, massive energy explosion finale.
💬

The dialogue line "Ok, are you ready?" has been written without special characters or capitals as required by Seedance 2.0 for best speech generation. Do not add punctuation marks around it.

🎬

Why so long? Seedance 2.0 reads the entire prompt before generating. The more specific your camera directions, action sequence, and audio cues, the more cinematic the result. Short prompts produce generic outputs — long, detailed prompts produce scenes like this.
