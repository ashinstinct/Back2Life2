Take or Choose Your Selfie Photo

The foundation of your character — get this right and the rest follows

1

Take a clear, well-lit selfie or portrait photo of yourself against a plain or simple background. Natural light near a window works perfectly.

2

Make sure your full face and upper body are visible. Avoid sunglasses, hats, or heavy shadows across your face.

3

Save it to your device. You'll upload this into Higgsfield.ai in the next step.

💡

Pro tip: A plain white or grey wall behind you gives Nano Banana Pro the cleanest result when generating your character sheet. Outdoors in soft daylight works great too.

2

Generate Your Character Sheet Using Nano Banana Pro (Inside Higgsfield)

Use Nano Banana Pro inside Higgsfield.ai — free unlimited generations with 2K quality

1

Log in to Higgsfield.ai. Tap Create Image. In the Model selector at the bottom, choose Nano Banana Pro. Toggle Unlimited to ON (the green toggle) for free unlimited generations. Set quality to 2K and format to 9:16 or 16:9.

2

Tap the + Reference images box and upload your selfie photo. Then paste the following prompt into the prompt box and tap Generate:

📋 Prompt — Step 1: Character Sheet from Selfie
Using the face and physical features from the uploaded photo, generate a professional AI character reference sheet on a clean white background. Show the same person in four poses arranged side by side: full front view, 45-degree side view, full back view, and 45-degree opposite side view. Also include a close-up face portrait in the top right corner. The person should be wearing casual everyday clothing — jeans, a white t-shirt and a simple overshirt. Keep the face, skin tone, hair colour, build and facial features as faithful to the reference photo as possible. Style: clean professional character design sheet, photorealistic quality, consistent lighting from all angles.
3

Wait for Nano Banana Pro to generate the result. If the face doesn't look quite right, hit Generate again and add to your prompt: "keep the face more faithful to the reference photo — same skin tone, same eye colour, same hair."

4

Once happy, click the Download button on your generation to save the character sheet. You'll upload this again in Step 3.

ℹ️

Your result should look like the example below — multiple angles of the same person on a white background with a face close-up. This is your permanent AI avatar for any future videos. Nano Banana Pro with Unlimited ON means you can regenerate as many times as needed at no cost.

Example Output — Character Sheet (Casual Clothes)

Swap Into the England Kit With Your Name & Number

Transform your character sheet into a personalised England football kit

1

In Higgsfield.ai, tap Create Image with Nano Banana Pro and upload the character sheet you just saved in Step 2.

2

Paste the prompt below — replacing [YOUR NAME] with your surname in capitals and [YOUR NUMBER] with your chosen squad number:

📋 Prompt — Step 2: Kit Swap to England Football Kit
Using the character reference sheet in the uploaded image, regenerate the exact same 4-angle character sheet plus face portrait, but now dress the character in the official England home football kit. The kit should be: white short-sleeved football shirt with the Three Lions England badge on the left chest, the number [YOUR NUMBER] printed in large navy blue on the front left, navy blue shorts with the Three Lions badge, white football socks pulled up to the knee, and black football boots. On the BACK of the shirt, print the name [YOUR NAME] in large bold navy blue capital letters above the number [YOUR NUMBER]. Keep all facial features, skin tone, hair, and build identical to the uploaded reference. White studio background. Photorealistic quality. Show all 4 angles so the name and number on the back are clearly visible in the rear-facing view.
3

Nano Banana Pro will generate your character dressed in the England kit. Check the rear-facing angle — your name and number should be clearly visible on the back of the shirt.

4

If the name or number text isn't quite right, generate again and add to your prompt: "Fix the text on the back — it should read [YOUR NAME] above [YOUR NUMBER] in bold navy blue capital letters."

5

Download this England kit character sheet from Higgsfield. This is the reference image you'll use when generating your video in Step 4.

⚡

Want a different kit? You can swap "England home football kit" for any team — Brazil yellow, Man United red, Real Madrid white. Just change the kit description in the prompt. The name/number swap works exactly the same way.

Generate Your Viral Video on Higgsfield

Feed your kit character sheet into Seedance 2.0 and bring the scene to life

1

Go to higgsfield.ai and log in or create a free account.

2

Click "Create" then select "Video" from the generation options.

3

In the model selector, choose Seedance 2.0 from the dropdown.

4

Click the image upload button (usually a paperclip or image icon) and upload your England kit character sheet from Step 3. Set its role to "Reference Image" or "Omni".

5

In the prompt box, paste the full video prompt below:

Pro Tips to Make It Go Viral

Small tweaks that make a huge difference to reach and engagement

🎯

Hook in the first 0.5 seconds: The sofa-to-screen jump is your hook. If you post on TikTok, trim the clip so the jump happens within the very first second — this stops the scroll.

🔊

Add match atmosphere audio: Download a royalty-free crowd roar + commentary audio and layer it over your video in CapCut or any editor. The audio sells the illusion completely.

📝

Caption your video: Use text overlay in CapCut showing the commentator lines — "WILLIAMS HAS GOT THE BALL... HE SCORES!" Text captions double watch time on TikTok.

🏆

Post during match days: Upload your video on actual England or World Cup match days for maximum hashtag reach. #EnglandFootball, #WorldCup2026, #AIVideo, #Back2Life

♻️

Reuse your character sheet: Save your England kit character sheet forever — you can use it for any future football video, different stadiums, different opponents. It's your permanent AI football avatar.
