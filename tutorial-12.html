Viral Car
And Coffee Scene

A single continuous interior car shot — a coffee-cup confrontation at a red light, then a Desert Eagle reveal that flips the whole mood — a two-scene viral driving sequence built with Dreamina Seedance 2 Mini.

Tool: Seedance 2 Mini
Quality: 720p · 9:16
Difficulty: Intermediate
Time: ~12 mins
Follow Instagram TikTok YouTube

What You'll Create

A 2-scene, 30-second continuous interior car sequence — never cutting outside the vehicle. Scene 1: driving fast through wet NYC streets, a hard stop, and a coffee cup exploding across the windshield mid-confrontation. Scene 2: the camera reveals a Desert Eagle resting on your lap, one calm line delivered through the lowered window, and the whole mood flips.

Prepare Your Reference Photo

A clear photo is the foundation this whole generation is built on

1

Choose a clear, well-lit photo of yourself (or whoever's appearing in the clip). Front-facing works best — avoid sunglasses or heavy shadows.

2

Turn it into a multi-angle reference sheet using GPT Image 2 — four angles plus a face close-up on a plain background, exactly like the one below. This is the same method used to build the reference sheet for this generation.

3

Open Dreamina AI and start a new Seedance 2 Mini image-to-video generation.

4

Upload your reference sheet as the reference / character image, and the Desert Eagle photo as a second reference for Scene 2.

Scene 1 — The Coffee Cup

📋 Scene 1 Prompt
Camera never leaves the inside of the car. No external shots. No cuts. Single continuous interior shot only. Everything filmed from inside a black 1969 Dodge Charger muscle car.

[0:00-0:05]
Extreme close-up filling the entire frame with the face of reference_1, white male early 40s, short cropped grey hair, stubble beard, black leather jacket, gothic cross stitching, rock graphic tee. He is driving aggressively at high speed through wet NYC midtown streets, both hands gripping the wheel hard, body lurching violently with the motion of the car, changing gears with his right hand. He glances directly into camera, face tense with adrenaline, and delivers his line frantic breathless erratic, words tumbling out fast, voice cracking with urgency, London British accent, panicked and intense: I am about to show you something that changes everything, thirty seconds that is all I need. Use AshLondonVoice.mp3 as the voice reference for this dialogue.

Camera floats slowly back in one smooth continuous move, still inside the car, settling into passenger seat position over his right shoulder. Dashboard visible bottom of frame. His shoulder and head visible left of frame. Through the windshield, wet NYC streets, yellow cabs, steam vents, and an armoured Brinks security truck visible ahead.

[0:05-0:08]
Traffic signal snaps red. Ash stamps the brakes with full force. Violent deceleration. Body thrown hard forward against seatbelt. Hands bracing on wheel. Tyres screaming heard from inside. Car shudders to a dead stop. Through the windshield a 50s male New Yorker in grey overcoat is standing in the crosswalk with his knees literally touching the front bumper. His face is white with shock. Both hands slam down hard on the bonnet, staring through the windshield at Ash in disbelief. Camera stays inside watching through the windshield. Red signal glow #FF2200 casting through the glass.

[0:08-0:11]
New Yorker explodes into pure rage. Grabs his Starbucks coffee cup, winds his arm back, and hurls it with full force at the windshield. Cup smashes the glass and explodes on impact. Milky white coffee sheets the entire windshield in one massive wave. Near total whiteout of view. Coffee running in thick rivulets down the glass, steam rising from hot liquid. Ash calmly reaches forward and switches on the windscreen wipers. Wipers begin slowly smearing the milky coffee left and right across the glass in wide arcs, gradually clearing the view.

[0:11-0:15]
Camera pans smoothly left inside the car. New Yorker has moved to the driver side window. Face pressed against the glass. Palm slamming the window repeatedly with full force. Screaming furious obscenities, face red, veins visible in neck, gesturing wildly. Ash in driver seat stares dead ahead, one hand on wheel, jaw clenched, completely still. Camera captures everything through the driver side window glass from inside.

Photorealistic cinematic. Interior shadow #1A1A1A. Dashboard warm glow #D4C5A0. Coffee brown fluid on windshield #6B3A1F. Red signal glow #FF2200.

Scene 2 — The Reveal

📋 Scene 2 Prompt — 15 Seconds, Direct Continuation Of Scene 1
Camera never leaves the inside of the car. No external shots. No cuts. Single continuous interior shot. Opens on exact continuation from start_image — inside black 1969 Dodge Charger, reference_1, white male early 40s, short cropped grey hair, stubble beard, black leather jacket, gothic cross stitching, rock graphic tee, black leather pants, in driver seat. Rain-soaked driver side window with furious 50s male New Yorker in grey suit outside, pressing his face against the glass mid-rant, screaming and slamming the window.

[0:00-0:03]
Dolly back reveal. Camera performs a slow smooth dolly backward, Ash's face centred in frame, shot widens gradually revealing shoulders, chest, torso, then his lap — a reference_2 Magnum Research Desert Eagle, brushed chrome and black large frame handgun, resting naturally on his right thigh, right hand gripped firmly around it, index finger on trigger. Not raised yet. Just resting there. Incidental. Menacing. Ash stares forward ignoring the New Yorker. New Yorker still ranting, muffled through the closed rain-soaked glass.

[0:03-0:05]
Electric window down, silence. Ash reaches across with one finger and presses the electric window button on the door panel beside the door handle. Window hums and begins dropping electrically. The instant the glass starts moving the New Yorker stops talking mid-sentence, completely cut off, silenced, freezes staring at Ash wrong-footed. Silence except for the electric window hum. Window fully down. Ash slowly turns his head and looks at the New Yorker. Dead calm. Says nothing. Holds eye contact.

[0:05-0:08]
The gun rises and the line. Ash slowly raises the Desert Eagle from his lap, lifts it upward in one slow deliberate motion, barrel pointing upward through the open window toward the sky, not pointed at the man, just raised and visible. The New Yorker's eyes track the gun rising from the lap upward. Locks onto it. Face drains completely white. Ash looks back at the New Yorker and delivers quietly, almost a whisper, this is Ash's own London British voice from the audio reference file AshLondonVoice.mp3, use only this voice, no other voice, no famous actor voice: today is not the day mate.

[0:08-0:11]
The reaction. Only now does the New Yorker react, completely different man, all aggression gone, eyes wide locked on the raised gun, pure terrified strong Irish accent stumbling panicked over every word: oh Jesus sure ting fella no bother at all you have a lovely day now. Hands raise slowly at his sides, palms outward. Steps back one pace. Nodding frantically. Forced terrified smile. Backing away slowly. Eyes never leaving the raised Desert Eagle.

[0:11-0:13]
Electric window up, the squeak. Ash holds eye contact one beat longer than comfortable. Lowers the gun back to his lap slowly. Reaches across with one finger and presses the electric window button. Window hums rising electrically. As the wet rubber seal meets the wet glass it creates a long slow awkward high-pitched squeak, drawn out, uncomfortable, the only sound filling the silence as the window seals shut. New Yorker frozen mid-nod watching it go up. Window seals shut. Complete silence.

[0:13-0:15]
Floor it. Traffic signal flips green. Ash looks forward. Both hands grip the wheel. Stamps the accelerator hard. Tyres spin and screech. Car launches violently forward through the intersection, leaving the New Yorker standing in the road staring after it. Through the rain-soaked windshield the armoured Brinks security truck visible ahead in the distance, still running. Camera stays inside.

Photorealistic cinematic. Interior shadow #1A1A1A. Rain on window glass. Dashboard warm glow #D4C5A0. Signal green #00FF41. NYC wet street reflections through glass. Shallow depth-of-field on Ash. New Yorker soft focus through glass.
🎬

The accent flip sells the whole scene — panicked London urgency in Scene 1, dead calm for the line, then the New Yorker's terrified Irish reaction. Keep AshLondonVoice.mp3 locked in for both of Ash's lines so the voice never drifts.
