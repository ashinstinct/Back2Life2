My 5 Year Old Self
In Music Video

Bring your childhood self into a glowing, magical adventure — running through an enchanted forest clutching a snow globe with a tiny volcano inside, using Dreamina Seedance 2 Mini.

Quality: 720p · 9:16
Difficulty: Beginner

What You'll Create

Take an old childhood photo and bring it to life — your younger self runs joyfully through a magical, glowing forest clutching a glass snow globe with a miniature erupting volcano inside. Warm embers and enchanted light stream between the trees. A wholesome, nostalgic hook that performs incredibly well as a music video insert.

Prepare Your Reference Photo

A clear photo is the foundation this whole generation is built on

1

Choose a clear, well-lit photo of yourself (or whoever's appearing in the clip). Front-facing works best — avoid sunglasses or heavy shadows.

2

Turn it into a multi-angle reference sheet using GPT Image 2 — four angles plus a face close-up on a plain background, exactly like the one below. This is the same method used to build the reference sheet for this generation.

3

Open Dreamina AI and start a new Seedance 2 Mini image-to-video generation.
