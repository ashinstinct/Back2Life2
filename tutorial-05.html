Choose a clear, well-lit photo of yourself (or whoever's appearing in the clip). Front-facing works best — avoid sunglasses or heavy shadows.

3

Open Dreamina AI and start a new Seedance 2 Mini image-to-video generation.

4

Upload your reference sheet as the reference / character image. This generation also uses three extra reference images — your room, the Meta Quest 3, and the game-selection UI — shown below.

📋 Scene 2 Prompt — Dojo Breach, 15 Sec Fight Sequence
Style: Hyperreal Pop, ultra-realistic, Shaolin Soccer aesthetic — comic book physics, impossible speed, exaggerated impact forces, motion blur trails, dust explosions, chi energy visible as shockwaves. Cinematography: handheld kinetic, snap zooms, speed ramping. Color grade: warm amber dojo tones crushed to high-contrast shadows, electric blue HUD glow from VR headset cutting through the dark. Location: traditional Japanese dojo interior. Hardwood floors with deep grain. Rice paper shoji screens. Red lacquered pillars. Hanging paper lanterns swaying from impact vibrations. Dust motes thick in afternoon light shafts cutting through smoke. No audience. Two fighters alone.

Characters:
Hoodie — slim athletic male, black hoodie with neon Back2Life.ai logo, beige cargo shorts, black and neon Nike Air Max 90s, Meta Quest 3 VR headset locked onto face with electric blue glowing lenses. Moves like water, low, fast, unpredictable. Shaolin kung fu style.
Sumo — enormous Japanese sumo wrestler, traditional mawashi belt, topknot, bare feet. Slow to turn but devastating when he lands. Shakes the floor with every step.

[0:00-0:01] Hard cut in
Extreme wide shot. Both fighters already locked in staring stance center dojo. Sumo stomps both feet simultaneously, shockwave cracks the floorboards, splinters the wood, lanterns swing violently. Hoodie drops into deep horse stance, VR lenses flickering blue. Dust explosion outward from stomp.

[0:01-0:03] The charge
Sumo thunders forward, each footstep cratering the floor, motion blur on his arms swinging wide. Hoodie explodes forward low, near ground level, arms tucked, hard cut to ground level angle as he hits a sliding baseball slide directly between the sumo's spread legs, passing completely underneath, Air Max 90s skidding across the wood. Speed lines streak behind him. Sumo's momentum carries him two steps past, he turns slowly, confused.

[0:03-0:05] The turn
Snap zoom onto sumo's face, fury. He spins and launches a thunderclap double hand slam downward toward Hoodie. Hoodie rolls sideways, the slam hitting the floor so hard a shockwave ripples the wood grain like water. Dust and splinters mushroom upward. Hoodie pops back to his feet behind the sumo.

[0:05-0:07] Body launch sequence
Hoodie breaks into a full sprint directly at the sumo's back. He leaps, planting his right foot on the back of the sumo's left knee, the knee acts as a launch ramp. Impossible Shaolin Soccer physics: he runs up the back of the sumo's body, foot on knee, foot on lower back, foot on shoulder blade, in three rapid staccato steps, momentum carrying him upward. Each footfall sends ripple shockwaves through the sumo's flesh. Speed ramp slow mo on the ascent.

[0:07-0:09] The backflip
At full height, standing on the sumo's shoulder, speed ramp slows to 10 percent, Hoodie plants both feet and launches a backflip off the sumo's body. Full tuck backflip. VR headset lenses glow bright white at the apex. Below him the sumo staggers forward from the force, arms windmilling. Mid air, Hoodie is silhouetted against the hanging lanterns. Time stretches. Dust hangs frozen.

[0:09-0:11] Airborne combo
Speed slams back to 100 percent. Hoodie lands in a crouch directly facing the sumo. Without pause he launches a rapid three hit combo, left jab to the gut, right elbow to the ribs, left spinning back kick to the thigh. Each hit produces a visible chi shockwave ripple radiating outward in Shaolin Soccer style. The sumo absorbs each blow, barely, but takes one step back with each hit, which for a sumo is seismic.

[0:11-0:13] The roundhouse
Hoodie plants his left foot, pivots, and unloads a full roundhouse kick aimed at the sumo's temple. Shaolin Soccer physics: the kick generates a visible compression wave of air, a sonic boom ring that shatters the nearest paper shoji screen. The sumo's head snaps sideways in slow motion. Cheeks ripple. Topknot whips. The lantern above explodes from the pressure wave. Sparks and burning paper rain down.

[0:13-0:15] Stillness
The sumo teeters. Hold on his enormous frame swaying in silence. One beat. Two beats. Then, timber, he falls forward in full slow motion, the floorboards splitting beneath his impact, dust and splinters erupting in a perfect circle. Hoodie stands over him, one foot on the ground, one raised in crane pose. VR lenses cool from white back to electric blue. Dust settles. A single lantern swings.

Hard cut to black.

Wardrobe continuity: black hoodie remains on throughout. Meta Quest 3 headset never leaves his face. Black and neon Nike Air Max 90s visible in every footwork shot. Sumo mawashi belt intact throughout.

SFX notes: bass heavy impact thuds. Shaolin Soccer style whoosh exaggeration on every strike. Shockwave sub bass on sumo stomps. Crackling wood. Shattering paper screens. Silence at apex of backflip.

Camera: handheld. Snap zooms on key impact moments. Ground level during slide sequence. Bird's eye at backflip apex. Slow motion, 10 percent, at backflip peak and roundhouse impact. Otherwise 24fps kinetic.
