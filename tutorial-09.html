My Reflection
Came Alive

Your own reflection slaps you awake, breaks the news about Seedance 2 Mini, then drags you through the glass — a 15-second comedic-horror UGC ad built with Seedance 2 Mini.

Tool: Seedance 2 Mini
Quality: 720p · 9:16
Difficulty: Advanced

What You'll Create

A four-beat bathroom-mirror UGC ad with a full comedic-horror arc. You slam both palms on the sink, hollow-eyed and exhausted — then your reflection reaches out and slaps you across the face to wake you up. It breaks the news about Seedance 2 Mini with genuine excitement, invites you to "come check it out," then grabs your arm and pulls you bodily through the mirror in a burst of shattering liquid glass.
